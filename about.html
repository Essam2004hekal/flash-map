About us

Who Us

FlashMap is a leading search engine and booking company for flights, transportation, hotels, apartments and holiday packages. In addition, we provide integrated services to develop and market destinations and loyalty programs for airlines, tour operators, destination management companies and hotels.


Our Massage

Facilitating travel experiences for travelers around the world by providing innovative and comprehensive booking solutions that meet all their needs and exceed their expectations.


Our vision

We aim to be the first choice for travelers around the world, and the trusted partner for airlines, hotels and tour operators.

  • Search and booking engine : Wide choice of flights at competitive prices, Booking land, sea and air transportation services.

  • Developing and marketing destinations : Integrated strategies for developing tourist destinations, Marketing campaigns targeting potential customers online

  • Loyalty programs : Programs designed to increase customer loyalty and enhance long-term relationships, Customized solutions to enhance guest satisfaction and increase guest return

  • Family Guide : It is a service dedicated to families and girls, aiming to provide an integrated, safe and enjoyable travel experience, specifically designed to meet the needs and desires of all family members.
